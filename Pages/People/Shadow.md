### Primary Prompt

A sharp, high-contrast photograph of a person's shadow cast on a sunlit, orange brick wall. The silhouette of the head and shoulders is well-defined. Strong, direct sunlight from a low angle. The texture of the bricks and the grid of the gray mortar lines are clearly visible. Minimalist, abstract, street photography.
